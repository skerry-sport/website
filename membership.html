Getting Started

As a new class association, we're still in the process of setting up our formal membership structure. Here's how you can get involved:

  1. Express Interest: Contact us to let us know you're interested in joining
  2. Stay Informed: We'll keep you updated on the first Annual General Meeting where membership dues and procedures will be established
  3. Join a Fleet: Look for local Skerry Sport fleets in your area or help start one
  4. Participate: Get involved in class discussions and help shape our young association

Fleet Formation

Any group of three or more eligible boats may form a Fleet. Fleets:

  • Elect a Fleet Captain and register with the Association
  • May establish local policies, provided they don't contradict Class Rules
  • Organize local racing and social events
  • Serve as the primary point of contact for local sailors

Annual Dues

Annual dues will be set by vote of the membership at our first Annual General Meeting (AGM). We're committed to keeping dues affordable and accessible to encourage broad participation in our class.
