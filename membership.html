Membership

Join our growing community of Skerry Sport sailors

Membership Types

Full Member

$25/year

  • Voting rights in association matters
  • Eligible to hold office
  • Access to member directory
  • Discounts on class events
  • Technical support from class measurer

Requirements: Own or actively sail a CLC Skerry

Associate Member

$15/year

  • Stay informed about class activities
  • Access to technical resources
  • Invitations to social events
  • Building support and advice

Perfect for builders and sailing enthusiasts

How to Join

  1. Choose your membership type - Full or Associate
  2. Complete the application - Available in our documents repository
  3. Submit payment - PayPal, check, or bank transfer options available
  4. Welcome! - You'll receive a confirmation and member information packet

Member Benefits

  • Technical Support: Access to class measurer and building experts
  • Events: Exclusive access to class championships and gatherings
  • Community: Connect with Skerry Sport sailors worldwide
  • Resources: Building tips, sailing techniques, and class news
  • Advocacy: Representation in sailing associations and events
