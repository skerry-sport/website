New to Skerry Sport?

If you're just learning about the Skerry Sport and considering building or buying one, here's how to get started:

  1. Learn about the boat: Visit Chesapeake Light Craft to see the design, plans, and kit options
  2. Connect with builders: Join online forums and communities to learn from experienced builders
  3. Consider your rig: The Skerry Sport offers three rig options - Balanced Lug, Sprit, and Sloop
  4. Join our community: Contact us to connect with other builders and sailors in your area

Volunteer Opportunities

As a new association, we need help with many activities:

  • Officer Positions: We'll be electing our first officers at our inaugural AGM
  • Technical Committee: Help develop measurement procedures and rule interpretations
  • Fleet Development: Assist with organizing and supporting new fleets
  • Event Organization: Help plan our first championship regatta and other events
  • Communications: Assist with website development, social media, and member communications

Stay Updated

While we're establishing our formal communication channels, here are the best ways to stay informed:

  • GitHub: Watch our repositories for updates and announcements
  • Website: Check back here regularly for news and updates
  • Community: Connect with other Skerry Sport enthusiasts through sailing forums and CLC communities
