About Chesapeake Light Craft

The Skerry Sport was designed by John Harris at Chesapeake Light Craft (CLC). While our association is independent, we maintain a close relationship with CLC and appreciate their support for the class.

CLC is located in Annapolis, Maryland, and has been designing and selling boat kits and plans for over 25 years. The Skerry Sport represents their expertise in creating beautiful, accessible boats for home builders.
