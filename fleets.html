Starting a New Fleet

Interested in starting a Skerry Sport fleet in your area? We'd love to help! Here's what you need:

Minimum Requirements

  • 3+ boats in your sailing area
  • 1 fleet captain to coordinate activities
  • Local sailing venue - yacht club, sailing association, or public waters
  • Interest in regular racing or sailing together

What We Provide

  • Official recognition as a SSODCA fleet
  • Technical support from class measurer
  • Racing handicaps and rule guidance
  • Promotional materials to attract new members
  • Connection to other fleets and the broader community

Fleet Benefits

  • Organize local races and events
  • Build community among local Skerry Sport sailors
  • Participate in class championships
  • Share building tips and sailing techniques
  • Welcome new builders and sailors to the class

Join the Movement

Whether you're building your first Skerry Sport or have been sailing one for years, we'd love to connect you with other sailors in your area.
