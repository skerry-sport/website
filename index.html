Welcome to Our New Class

The Skerry Sport One-Design Class Association (SSODCA) is a nascent sailing class just getting started. We're excited to bring together owners and sailors of boats built from the Skerry kit or plans as published by Chesapeake Light Craft.

What is a Skerry Sport?

The Skerry Sport is a 15-foot, 4.5-beam double-ended sailboat featuring beautiful glued-lapstrake plywood construction. Available in three rig configurations - Balanced Lug, Sprit, and Sloop - the Skerry Sport offers exciting sailing performance in a package that's accessible to home builders.

Why Join Our Class?

  • Fair Racing: Our one-design rules ensure that sailing skill, not boat modifications, determines race results
  • Multiple Rig Options: Race in Balanced Lug, Sprit, or Sloop configurations with appropriate handicaps
  • Builder-Friendly: We understand that home-built boats will have natural variations and account for this in our rules
  • Growing Community: Be part of establishing a new class with sailors who share your passion for these beautiful boats

Ready to Get Started?

Whether you're building your first Skerry Sport or already sailing one, we'd love to have you join our association.
